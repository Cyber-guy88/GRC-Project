2018 Aadhaar Data Breach: India's Digital Identity Crisis

June 15, 2023 8 min read By GRC Expert Team

The 2018 Aadhaar data breach exposed vulnerabilities in India's national biometric ID system, raising critical questions about data security and privacy protections for over 1.1 billion citizens.

Breach Overview

  • Discovery Date: January 2018
  • Reported By: The Tribune (Indian newspaper)

  • Affected: 1.1 billion Indian residents

Compromised Data

  • Aadhaar numbers
  • Full names
  • Residential addresses
  • Contact numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Linked service information (bank accounts, mobile numbers)

Biometric data (fingerprints/iris scans) remained secure

Key Impact Areas

Security Failures

Unauthorized access through compromised vendor credentials

Privacy Concerns

Mass exposure of PII (Personally Identifiable Information)

Legal Repercussions

Supreme Court interventions and updated data protection laws

Post-Breach Reforms

  • Introduction of Virtual ID (VID) system
  • Strict access control protocols
  • Mandatory security audits
  • Amendments to Aadhaar Act (2019)
