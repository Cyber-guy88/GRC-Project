The S3-4D Approach

Infosys has developed a comprehensive approach to Governance, Risk, and Compliance that combines security assurance (S3) with digital transformation (4D). This framework enables organizations to build a security foundation that scales with their business, prepares for future challenges, and delivers digital-first solutions across the GRC lifecycle.
